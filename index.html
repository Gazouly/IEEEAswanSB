IEEE Aswan Student Branch (IEEE Aswan SB) was founded in the academic year (2010-2011). It is located at the Faculty of Engineering and serves all departments within the faculty, but its services are not limited to these departments as it provides all students with different soft skills sessions and training.

Benefits Of Membership

IEEE E-Learning Library

It is a series of online learning tutorials based on educational content from IEEE conferences around the world. These tutorials have been developed by experts of engineering and research technologies.

IEEE E-Book Classics

IEEE members now have access to more than 250 eBooks from the IEEE Press collection through IEEE Xplore. The eBook collection spans a number of today's technologies across 15 different content areas.

IEEE Student Competitions

Competitions develop students professionally and technically. Many of them, like IEEEXtreme and Student paper contest, provide a way for student members to connect to the global IEEE community.
